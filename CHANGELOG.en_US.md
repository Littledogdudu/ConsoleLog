## [1.1.8] - 2026-2-21

- Add console.log, dir, table, and other options (shortcut: Ctrl+Alt+1, use template selection) (proposed by cscsyiku123)
- console.table only supports a single data parameter. Since there is no additional identifier for recognition, one-click delete, comment, and uncomment cannot distinguish whether it was generated by the plugin or added manually. You can configure in the settings whether to enable one-click delete, comment, and uncomment specifically for console.table.
